## Critical Laws

1. AWRAG is a proving lane for AnchorWorks, not a chatbot, normal RAG stack, or LLM wrapper.
2. The system is the admitted data. Evidence authority comes from citations, coordinates, native rank keys, support classes, and receipts.
3. Do not mutate counts, citations, coordinates, runtime data, or lifetime memory unless explicitly asked.
4. Keep evidence traces separate from readable/pretty answers.
5. Do not introduce model authority, embeddings, rerankers, hidden scoring, or silent behavior changes.
6. Adjacent systems such as SecureCore, TrueVision, and TrueAudio may be documented here only when clearly trapped; do not wire them into AWRAG runtime without explicit approval.
7. Refactor means same behavior with cleaner structure. It does not mean new features, scoring changes, intake rule changes, or backend redesign.
8. If evidence is absent, say support is absent. If evidence exists, show the path.

Short law:

```text
Frontend/operator surfaces display.
Backend/tool boundaries translate.
AWRAG decides from admitted evidence.
Receipts prove what happened.
```
